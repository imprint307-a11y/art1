Artist Statement

Why I create art.

I create art to translate inner emotions into visual forms that can be seen, felt, and remembered. My practice is rooted in observation, sensitivity, and the belief that images can express what words often cannot. Through color, texture, and composition, I explore identity, memory, human connection, and the quiet tension between reality and imagination. As an emerging artist, I see every work as a step toward a clearer voice — one that speaks with honesty, beauty, and purpose.
